About

All About PizzaCuy

Our Brand Vision

To be the favourite casual dining place for families and friends to gather for great food and great time together, as it is affordable and offers a memorable service experience that makes one wants to return.

Our Passion

Starting in 1969 with our first outlet at Jalan Sukuh Cottage, PizzaCuy is now the largest pizza chain restaurant with 109 outlets. We have an unparalleled passion for celebrating our pizza. We aim to provide not only a wide variety of quality pizzas, pasta and other foods that are always fresh, but also a fun and friendly relaxed atmosphere. All this without compromising what a good restaurant always stands for customer service and satisfaction.

Over the years, our service, menu offerings and ambiance have evolved with the times and trends. Since then, we have embraced the concept of affordable casual dining with an emphasis on creating a fun and engaging atmosphere for our customers. PizzaCuy is a favorite place if you want to celebrate a special occasion, or just chill with friends and loved ones. We aim to make every dining experience a truly memorable one for our customers.
